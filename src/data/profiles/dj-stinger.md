DJ Stinger is a rising star in both the radio and club scenes, known for his high-energy sets and crowd-pleasing playlists. He spins a mix of chart-topping hits and classic bangers, creating an electric atmosphere wherever he performs.
Catch him on 91.2 Crooze FM, on two popular shows: The Morning Addiction on Fridays from 6:00 AM till 10:00 AM and The Urban Breakfast on Saturdays from 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM as he brings the party to your living rooms!

Stinger is also a resident DJ at Shooters Bar & Grill. He graduated in 2023 with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, DJ Stinger combines his passion for music with a sharp business mindset, making him one of the most versatile talents in the industry.

Whether on-air or in the club, DJ Stinger always ensures an unforgettable experience.
