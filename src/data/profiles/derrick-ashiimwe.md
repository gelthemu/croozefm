Passionate about a diverse mix of interests, I thrive on sports, hip-hop, the essential oxygen, and the vibrant world of radio. Interestingly, I share a birthday with the iconic Michael Jackson, though time zones place our actual birthdates on different days.

You can tune in to my voice on “The Morning Addiction,” where I deliver the English sports updates every Monday through Thursday at 7:40 AM. On Saturdays, join me and my co-hosts Abou Champion and Okello for “The Weekly Sports Round-Up Show,” airing from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM, where we dive deep into sports analysis, covering tennis, basketball, Formula One racing, and of course, football—my first love.

With an expansive knowledge in both local and international football, I bring a unique perspective to every discussion. As Nas famously said, “I never sleep, ’cause sleep is the cousin of death.” These words resonate with my relentless pursuit of passion and knowledge.

Cheers to connecting over shared enthusiasms and insightful conversations.
