**Mbarara, Uganda** – Western Uganda's Biggest Radio Station brings you the Crooze FM Weekly Mixtape every Wednesday. This [series](/c/mixtapes) was created to keep audiences dancing and sharing the excitement throughout the week.

On March 5, 2025, Crooze FM released its newest Weekly Mixtape, expertly mashed up by the talented Starcent DJ. The mixtape features an electrifying mix of the hottest jams, reflecting the station’s commitment to delivering “Great Music, for Great Friends.”

<section className="latest-release"></section>

The Weekly Mixtapes have become a key part of the local culture, offering fun and engagement. As Crooze FM keeps educating and entertaining its audience, these mixtapes play an essential role in its mission, making sure the rhythm of Mbarara and beyond stays strong and steady.

Whether enjoyed live or saved for later, these mixtapes are a testament to Crooze FM’s dedication to keeping the vibe alive in Western Uganda.
