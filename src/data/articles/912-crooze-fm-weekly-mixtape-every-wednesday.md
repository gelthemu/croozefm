**Mbarara, Uganda** – Western Uganda's Biggest Radio Station brings you the Crooze FM Weekly Mixtape every Wednesday. This [series](/c/mixtapes) was created to keep audiences dancing and sharing the excitement throughout the week.

On March 12, 2025, Crooze FM released its newest Weekly Mixtape, expertly mashed up by the talented DJ Nati J. The mixtape features an electrifying mix of the hottest jams, reflecting the station’s commitment to delivering “Great Music, for Great Friends.”
